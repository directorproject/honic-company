## About HonicLearn

HonicLearn is an education-focused innovation department established to bridge the gap between theoretical education and hands-on, practical learning in Tanzania. Operating under Honic Company Limited, HonicLearn designs and manufactures curriculum-based practical learning kits and skills development tools in Science, Engineering, and Technology.

By providing practical, hands-on educational tools, HonicLearn transforms students from passive learners into confident creators and problem-solvers, nurturing practical intelligence, creativity, and technical skills that empower learners to tackle real-world challenges.

---

## Background & History

HonicLearn was established to address a major gap in Tanzania's education system, where learning remains heavily theory-based due to limited access to practical learning tools. Many schools face shortages of laboratory equipment, training kits, and hands-on instructional materials, making it challenging for students to acquire practical skills, particularly in Science, Engineering, and Technology.

Recognizing this challenge, Honic Company Limited, an innovation-driven organization focused on product development, initiated HonicLearn as a dedicated education innovation department. Its goal is to design and locally manufacture curriculum-aligned, inclusive, high-quality practical learning tools with a strong focus on skills-based development, enabling students to actively engage in learning and apply knowledge in real-world contexts.

Officially established on **1st March 2025**, HonicLearn promotes hands-on, skills-based learning in line with the Tanzania Education and Training Policy (ETP) 2014, 2023 Edition (Elimu Amali). Our approach prepares students for practical problem-solving, innovation, and future employment. Since its inception, HonicLearn has collaborated with educational institutions, suppliers, and local partners to deliver practical STEM learning kits, conduct technical workshops, and enhance schools' capacity for hands-on education across Tanzania.

## Products & Services

HonicLearn provides a range of practical learning solutions and support services designed to enhance hands-on education, skills development, and innovation:

### 1. Research & Development (R&D)

HonicLearn undertakes comprehensive research and project implementation to address diverse educational challenges, including:

- Identifying gaps in practical and skills-based learning
- Developing innovative solutions and educational tools
- Testing and implementing projects in schools and communities
- Providing insights and strategies to improve learning outcomes across all education levels

### 2. Workshops & Training

Interactive sessions for students and teachers to develop practical skills, technical competencies, and problem-solving abilities, ensuring effective use of HonicLearn kits and tools.

### 3. Maintenance Services

Ongoing technical support and maintenance for practical kits and educational tools, ensuring sustainable, long-term usability and consistent hands-on learning.

### 4. Learning Kits

Curriculum-aligned STEM and skills development kits in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, enabling students to apply theoretical knowledge through practical experiments and exercises.

### 5. Inclusive Learning Digital Programs

Specialized programs for students with disabilities and underrepresented groups, including accessible kits, audio guidance, and video sign language instructions, promoting equitable participation in practical, skills-based education.

## Achievements & Impact

Since its establishment in March 2025, HonicLearn has achieved significant milestones:

- **Institutional Reach:** Serving 19 educational institutions, including 16 secondary schools and 3 universities, impacting over 1,500 students.
- **STEM Kits Delivered:** Over 500 practical and skills-development kits distributed.
- **Workshops & Training:** Conducted 3 technical workshops involving 189 students.
- **Maintenance & Support:** Provided support for 37 practical kits in secondary schools.
- **Partnerships:** Collaborated with 5 suppliers to strengthen local production and innovation.

**Growth Target:** HonicLearn aims to reach **10,000+ students by 2027**, expanding access to practical, hands-on, and skills-based education across Tanzania.
